# Lorenz-63 symbolic rediscovery benchmark

**System:** σ=10, ρ=28, β=8/3 (canonical chaotic regime)
**Trajectory:** T=30, dt=0.01 (~3000 RK4 samples post-transient)
**Observed:** all of x, y, z; finite-difference derivatives.
**GP:** pop=120, gens=40, parsimony=0.5

## Analytical truth
```
dx/dt = σ (y − x)              = 10 y - 10 x
dy/dt = x (ρ − z) − y          = 28 x - x z - y
dz/dt = x y − β z              = x y - (8/3) z
```
